Astronomy 4

Section 1 (M through F, 8:30-9:20 am)

Images from the OpenStax Astronomy book

If you’re using the OpenStax Astronomy text, you may find that the images in it are rather small.

Happily, though, larger versions of most of them can be found on the web.

The links below will take you to webpages that have larger versions of the images. You may have to do a little more “clicking through”, and/or scrolling down, to find the bigger image files. But in each case, the link will take you to a webpage that leads to one or more high-resolution versions of the image in question.

I’ve included some extra links to *other* high-quality versions of the images, or to alternative and/or related versions. These alternative links are commonly listed like this: “04_25_2”. This would mean that it’s another, second alternative to the main version of figure 25 in Chapter 4.

Not all images have links here. That’s usually for one of two reasons: 1) The authors drew the image themselves, or 2) I just couldn’t find a good link.

The figure numbers are based on the PDF version of the book that existed during the summer of 2017.
