Whenever you’re using these practice tests, here are some things you should keep in mind:

1) The `break’ between, say, Test #1 and Test #2 might have come at a different point in the schedule when I made these practice tests! For example, if the Test 1 - 2 break occurred SOONER when I wrote these old tests, you could get `trapped’ into not studying something that you should study! You may need to look at more than one practice test in order to see sample questions for all of your material.

So, to avoid this trap, make sure to look carefully at the What2Know page. This is always your guide to what’s on the test.

2) It’s possible that I might have covered certain subjects this quarter that I hadn’t covered when I wrote the practice test. There will always be some subjects that don’t show up on the practice test! Don’t assume that `it won’t be on our test, since it wasn’t on the practice test’ - that’s a potential trap! Always check the What2Know list.
